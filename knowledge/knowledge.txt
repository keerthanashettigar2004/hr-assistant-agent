

 1. Leave Policies
- Casual Leave: 12 days per year
- Sick Leave: 10 days per year
- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
- Paternity Leave: 2 weeks
- Apply via HR portal at least 2 days before leave
- Unpaid leave requires manager approval

2. Work Hours & Attendance
- Standard work hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM
- Flexible work timing: allowed with manager approval
- Remote work: max 2 days per week
- Late arrival/early departure: must be logged in attendance system
- Public holidays: as per company calendar

3. Recruitment & Onboarding
- Job openings posted on internal portal
- Resume submission and interview scheduling through HR
- Onboarding includes orientation, document submission, and system access
- Probation period: 6 months
- Confirmation review after probation

4. Employee Benefits
- Health Insurance: coverage up to ₹5,00,000 per annum
- Family members can be added to health insurance
- Retirement Fund: 12% of salary contributed monthly
- Employee wellness programs: yoga, gym, annual health checkup
- Travel allowance: as per company policy

 5. Payroll & Salary
- Payroll processed monthly
- Salary credited on 30th of each month
- Income tax deducted at source
- Bonus/Incentives: annual performance-based
- Payroll queries: contact HR/payroll team

6. IT & Tools
- Software/Hardware requests: submit ticket to IT portal
- Internal tools: email, HR portal, task tracker
- Password reset: self-service portal or IT helpdesk
- VPN access: required for remote work

7. Performance & Appraisal
- Performance review twice a year
- Appraisal based on KPIs, project delivery, and feedback
- Promotions: after at least 1 year in current role
- Feedback: continuous, via 1-on-1 meetings

 8. FAQs
- Q: How do I apply for leave?  
  A: Login to HR portal → Leave → Apply Leave → Submit.

- Q: How to claim medical reimbursement?  
  A: Submit claim form + bills to HR → HR processes within 15 days.

- Q: Where to find company forms?  
  A: All forms available in HR portal → Resources → Forms.

- Q: Who to contact for IT issues?  
  A: IT helpdesk via email or portal ticket system.

- Q: How to update personal details?  
  A: HR portal → Profile → Edit details → Submit for approval.
